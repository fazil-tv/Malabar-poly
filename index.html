Chairman & Principal Message

Mr. Sayyid Sadiq Ali Shihab Thangal

Chairman

Malabar Educational and Charitable Trust is a joint venture promoted and patronized by dedicated leaders and great scholars. The main objective of the trust is to provide better education to the society, to mould progressive younger genration and to make strong and developed India. Soon after the trust was registered under the Indian trust act 1882 in Sub Registrar office Cherpulassery on 24th February 2010, great efforts were made to fulfill the objectives of the trust.
